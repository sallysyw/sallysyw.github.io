A collection of Chinese poem translations, with Kevin Zhou :)

  • 长寿山居元夕 (Lantern Festival in Longevity Mountain)

    金朝·元好问(Yuan, Haowen)  · 03 Apr 2023

    Of thirty nine years of unending change,
    only my shadow at twilight remains.

  • 白头吟 (Song of Unwavering Love)

    两汉·卓文君(Zhuo, Wenjun)  · 14 Mar 2023

    I hope to find that pure of heart
    to stay with until white hair grows.

    in deepest night still climbs inside
